About Reben

Six decades of fueling the Kingdom forward.

Reben is a publicly listed joint-stock company on the Saudi Exchange, operating two major divisions: Petroleum Services and Transport & Logistics. We serve millions of customers daily across 14 cities with 127 stations and a modern fleet of 54 vehicles.

Our Mission

Deliver reliable, safe, and high-quality petroleum products to every corner of the Kingdom.

Our Vision

Be the most trusted fuel and logistics partner in the GCC by 2030.

Sustainability

Aligning with Saudi Vision 2030 through green infrastructure and responsible operations.

Safety First

Zero-incident culture with ADR-certified drivers and regular safety audits.
